CPT (Carriage Paid To)

The seller is responsible for loading the goods on the vessel chosen by the buyer, clearing the goods for export at the port/airport of loading, and paying freight costs to the named destination. The risk passes to the buyer once the goods are loaded on the first carrier. CPT can be used for any means of transport and should replace CFR when shipping containerised cargo.
